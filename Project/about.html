Who are Furry friends?

Furry Friends was established in 1997 right here in County Wicklow. We began with the aim of creating a safe and educational space for dogs and their owners. Throughout the years we have grown so much as a company from a humble beginning in a small, rented space to our brand-new custom-built location with all the necessary facilities for you and your dog.
In addition to providing these services we also offer many different educational days throughout the year which are regularly advertised on our social media pages!
